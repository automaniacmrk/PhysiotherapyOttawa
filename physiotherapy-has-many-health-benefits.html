Physiotherapy has many health benefits

PT helps the patients suffering from certain movement disorders, which could be congenital (present at the time of birth), age-related, accidental, or due to certain lifestyle changes.

Physiotherapists Ottawa work in collaboration with their patients by advising or guiding them about certain exercises and educating them about certain lifestyle changes, which altogether help alleviating various painful and impaired mobility conditions. Physiotherapists also promote movements such as walking and provide certain mobility aids, which can help the patients in improving their ailments.

The hands of physiotherapists are able to relieve pain and stiffness. They can also improve blood circulation, and relax the area. Manual therapy is a technique that mobilizes, manipulates, and improves the function of affected areas.

Graded Exercise Programmes

A graded exercise program can help you build strength, stamina and mobility. Your physiotherapist will show you how to start gently and then gradually increase your activity, without straining yourself or adding to your pain.

A combination of these options is likely to be recommended by your physiotherapist.

  • stretching exercises to help ease aches and pains and get the best movement from your joints
  • Strengthening exercises for strengthening the muscles supporting your joints
  • general fitness exercises, which are important for your general health
  • Proprioceptive exercises are a way to improve agility, balance and coordination.

Your physiotherapist can advise you on local classes such as yoga or t'ai chi, as well as walking and sports groups.

Hydrotherapy is a form of aquatic therapy that allows you to do exercises in warm water. Some physiotherapists also have access to this pool. Many people find it easier to move in water - the warmth is soothing and the water supports your weight so that you can move your joints and muscles without straining them.
